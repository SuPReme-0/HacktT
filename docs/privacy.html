Privacy Policy & Data Operation

Last updated: March 2026

1. Zero-Knowledge Cloud Policy

HackT Runtime is a sovereign intelligence agent. All AI reasoning (Qwen 3.5), threat detection, and vault storage occur strictly on your local hardware. We do not transmit your conversations, code snippets, or threat intel to our servers.

2. System Permissions & Local Processing

To function as an active security monitor, the HackT desktop application requests specific Windows permissions. This data never leaves your machine:

  • Screen Capture (OCR): Used by our local Vision model to read your IDE or browser for real-time threat detection.
  • Microphone Access: Used strictly for local speech-to-text (STT) voice commands.
  • Network Proxy: Used to scan local web traffic for phishing and vulnerabilities.

3. Data Collection (Authentication Only)

To secure your local Knowledge Vault and verify your identity, we use Google Single Sign-On (SSO) and Supabase Auth. We collect and store ONLY the following data in our secured cloud database:

  • Your Email Address
  • Your Public Google Profile Name
  • Your Public Google Avatar URL
